Our Story

A Legacy of Light & Craft

Founded in 2009, Elite Jewel Studio began as a small atelier with one promise — that every piece leaving our doors should be worthy of becoming a family heirloom. Today, three generations of artisans continue that tradition.

Mission

To craft jewellery that captures memories, honours culture, and elevates everyday moments into timeless ones.

Vision

To be South Asia's most trusted name in heritage and contemporary luxury jewellery.

Values

Craftsmanship · Integrity · Beauty · Generosity · Care for every customer.
